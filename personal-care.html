Quick Personal Care Tips for Busy Students

1

Set Reminders: Use phone alarms for important hygiene tasks you might forget during busy days.

2

Keep Essentials Handy: Carry hand sanitizer, tissues, and lip balm in your bag for on-the-go care.

3

Night Routine: Establish a simple 5-minute bedtime routine - it's easier to maintain than complex ones.

4

Hydrate: Drinking enough water is one of the best things you can do for your skin and overall health.

5

Sleep Well: Good sleep is essential for skin health and overall appearance. Aim for 7-8 hours.

6

Batch Tasks: Do weekly grooming tasks (nail care, hair masks) on the same day to save time.
