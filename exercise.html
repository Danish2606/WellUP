Exercise Tips for Students

1

Start Small

Begin with 10-15 minute sessions and gradually increase. Consistency beats intensity when building habits.

2

Schedule It

Treat exercise like a class. Block time in your calendar and stick to it.

3

Find a Buddy

Exercise with friends for accountability and motivation. It's more fun and you're less likely to skip.

4

No Equipment Needed

Bodyweight exercises are free, effective, and can be done anywhere. Push-ups, squats, and planks go a long way.

5

Mix It Up

Vary your routines to prevent boredom and work different muscle groups. Try cardio, strength, and flexibility exercises.

6

Listen to Your Body

Rest when needed. Recovery is crucial for progress and injury prevention.
